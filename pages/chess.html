About Game of Chess

Game of Chess is simple chess program that is playable against computer AI opponent or a second player.
Package also includes a manual on how to program your own chess program. Chess program guides you on how
to customize pieces and change the GUI. Software runs on game engine that support computer AI to play at
beginner or advance difficulty.
